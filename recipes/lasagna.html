Lasagna

Lasagna is such a great pasta dish. This recipe uses oven-ready, no cook lasagna noodles. If you make a real meaty sauce, you might want to use precooked lasagna noodles instead.

Ingredients

  • 10 Sheets of Lasagna noodles (oven ready)
  • 1lb minced meat (ground beef or a mic of beef and pork)
  • 1 large jar premade marinara sauce
  • 1 container cherry tomatoes
  • 16 oz
  • 2 peppers (any color, doesn't matter too much)
  • 2 T butter
  • 1 medium onion, diced finely
  • 1 c grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove of garlic, finely chopped
  • Glass of white wine, coffee (both optional)
  • Bechamel sauce
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 T butter
  • 2 T flour
  • pinch nutmeg

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, brown the onions in oil along with your garlic.
  2. Add your meat to the mixture and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Once meat is almost fully cooked, season this mixture to taste with salt and pepper.
  4. Add 3 tablespoons of freshly brewed coffee and 1 glass of white wine was also added.
  5. Cook until wine has fully evaporated.
  6. Mix Sauce
  7. Add mushrooms and enough tomato sauce make the sauce fairly liquid
  8. Mix thoroughly
  9. Add peppers and extra tomatoes to the tomato sauce and mix again.
  10. Set aside on low heat.
  11. Make the Bechamel sauce in another pot.
  12. In a pot, bring milk to a boil.
  13. In another pot, melt the butter. Add flour and pour in the hot milk. Be sure to whisk immediately so as not to form any clumps of flour.
  14. When mixture boils, remove heat and add nutmeg and salt to taste.
  15. Assemble
  16. Grease the edges of an oven dish.
  17. Line the bottom with a layer of bechamel sauce.
  18. Piece two sheets of lasagne on top. Then add a layer of the meat sauce.
  19. Add another layer of bechamel sauce, and sprinkle some parmesan cheese on top.
  20. Repeat for enough layers to fill the oven dish.
  21. Bake in oven for 30 minutes at 350.
