Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Home

Description

Nothing beats the simple joy of a perfectly toasted grilled cheese sandwich

Featuring thick-cut sourdough and a blend of sharp cheddar and creamy Havarti, this recipe focuses on the "low and slow" method to ensure a deeply golden, shatteringly crisp exterior and a center of pure, melted gold.

Whether you prefer the classic buttered crunch or the tangy secret of a mayonnaise-slathered crust, this guide will help you achieve the ultimate cheese pull every single time.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side each bread slice.
  3. Place 1 slice bread, butter-side down, in the hot skillet; top with 1 slice cheese.
  4. Place 1 slice bread, butter-side up, on top cheese.
  5. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  6. Repeat with remaining bread and cheese slices. Serve and enjoy!
